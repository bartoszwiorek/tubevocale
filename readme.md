# TubeVocale

`Warning: Currently in development`

## Short description

Easily switch YouTube video audio to your preferred language without affecting the site interface.

## Full Description:

Enhance your YouTube experience with the YouTube Audio Language Switcher! This lightweight extension adds a convenient dropdown menu at the bottom left of the YouTube player, allowing you to quickly change the video’s audio language on the fly.

## Key Features:

- Audio-Only Language Switching: Change the video’s voice/audio without altering the website’s interface language.
- Easy Access: Dropdown menu integrated seamlessly at the bottom left of the player.
- Quick & Intuitive: Switch languages instantly while watching videos.
- Lightweight & Fast: Minimal impact on browsing performance.
- Perfect for language learners, bilingual viewers, or anyone who wants to enjoy YouTube videos in multiple languages effortlessly.
